College and Career Readiness

Being college and career ready means that you graduate from high school with a career goal and a plan for how to get there. Scroll down to find resources that will help you get started determining your interests and learning about the jobs available in the San Diego economy. Here's what you can find...

1

Get work experience.

Use our jobs and internships board to find entry-level positions in your field of interest and apply today!

2

What problems do you want to solve?

Take a Career Assessment to determine which occupations align with your skills and interests and what types of work environments will empower you to succeed.

3

Explore careers.

Use the Labor Market resources below to learn about the diverse industry sectors that comprise San Diego's economy and the skills that employers need.

4

Education and Training

Make sure you're prepared to graduate college, are career ready, and know the types and levels of education required to enter various occupations.
