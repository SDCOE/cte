COVID-19 Resources for CTE Programs

Supporting high quality CTE in a Covid-19 impacted school year

The following resources and opportunities are curated and designed to support your CTE programs in distance, blended and socially distanced learning environments. 


The SDCOE College and Career Readiness team supports Career Technical Education and Career Readiness Initatives through our networks and work-based learning initaitve.  

Reopening Guidance and Distance Learning Resources

Utilize local, state and national guidance and resources to ensure high quality teaching and learning no matter the environment, and a safe reopening for staff and students.  See below for more information. 

CTE Instructors Network

Instructional strategies for distance and blended learning in CTE, sector and pathway specific resources, and opportunities for collaboration are all essential to providing high quality CTE programs.  See below for upcoming offerings. 

CTE Leaders Network

Focused on sharing of information and best practices, the CTE Leaders network provides a community for those commited to providing high quality CTE in the San Diego region. Email Jewyl Alderson Clarke for more information.

Virtual Work-Based Learning and Industry Connections

Ensure students still have access to industry professionals through synchronous and asyncrhonous virtual work-based learning opportunities.
