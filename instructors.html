CTE Instructors Network

With a focus on instructional practice for distance learning, blended learning, and socially distant learning, the CTE Instructors Network will be focused on instructional practices, and collaboration including planning, sector specific collaboration, student engagement, assessment, accessibility and networking.

Our fall offerings will include webinars, meetups and professional learning opportunities supporting CTE instructors across content areas.
