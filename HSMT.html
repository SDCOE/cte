Health Science and Medical Technology

Guest Speaker Presentations

County of San Diego - Health and Human Services Agency

View

HHSA provides a broad range of health and social services to promote wellness and self-sufficiency in SD. The TBEI team will share the challenges they face in stopping the spread of Tuberculosis (TB).

Thermo Fisher Scientific

View

Thermo Fisher is a global leader in designing and developing health science instruments, lab supplies, software, and professional services. Recorded by the community colleges, this panel features members of their team representing different roles and departments. 

HHSA - Covid 19 Facts for Students

View

Learn how to identify symptoms, discover if your community is in a COVID-19 “hot spot”, see where the new testing sites are, and gain a better understanding of San Diego County’s Public Health Orders.

Illumina

View

A global leader in genomics, designing and manufacturing instruments and systems for sequencing genomes, and analyzing genetic variations. They use information from DNA to help solve real world problems like cancer, genetic diseases, disease outbreaks and more.

Sharp HealthCare

View

Sharp is a SD healthcare company with 7 hospitals, 3 affiliated medical groups, over 2,600 physicians, and over 18,000 employees. Recorded by the community colleges, this presentation features 2 Sharp professionals discussing careers in nursing.

Illumina Career Profiles

View

Illumina created a series of career videos to highlight the diverse career opportunities in the field of genomics. Seven employees share their day-to-day schedule, what they work on, and who they work with.

Dr. Elizabeth Stanton

View

Psychology is often limited to the idea of therapy, but Dr. Stanton, a licensed psychologist who serves individuals, families, and couples shares the opportunities in forensics, abnormal psychology, and public health.
