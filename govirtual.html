Educator Experience  

ARCHIVE

The main conference is focused on high quality instruction in a Covid-19 impacted school year, with sessions on EdTech, Industry related curriculum, post-secondary alignment, SEL and more!

The conference is designed for educators, including classroom teachers, resource teachers, counselors, administrators, community/industry partners and after school expanded learning programming staff.

These sessions WILL BE RECORDED. You will find a link to the archives on this page within a few weeks of the conference. The archives will be publicly available to atttendees and all other stakeholders. 

Student Experience

ARCHIVE

The students experience focuses on career readiness, post-secondary options and work-based learning. 

The “Student Experience” is designed for students of attending classroom educators in San Diego County. More information will be sent once you register.  

Students can be registered by their teacher and submit a digital permission slip in order to have choice between a number of sessions and to engage in the Q&A sessions.

Unregistered students or students without a permission slip can participate in select conference breakouts each day through our student experience live stream!
