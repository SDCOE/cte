About Us

Career pathways are an integral part of a student’s preparation for life after high school.

SDCOE's College and Career Readiness team supports the delivery of exemplary career education in San Diego County K-12 schools with the goal that every student graduates prepared for college, career and beyond. Our services bridge the educational experience with the world of work so that students can explore careers, build technical and professional skills, and create a blueprint for their future.
